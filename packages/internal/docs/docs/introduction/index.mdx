# What is SaaS Boilerplate?

<ProjectName /> is a **production-ready codebase** that dramatically reduces the time your team spends building common SaaS functionality. Instead of reinventing the wheel, you can reuse battle-tested components and focus on what makes your product unique.

:::tip Time Savings
Most teams report saving **3-6 months** of development time by starting with SaaS Boilerplate instead of building from scratch.
:::
